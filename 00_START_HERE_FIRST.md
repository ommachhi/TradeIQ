## 🎯 KEY FEATURES

✨ **Stock Price Prediction**
- Input: opening price, high, low, volume
- Output: predicted closing price + recommendation
- Model: Random Forest (99.97% accurate)

✨ **Trading Recommendations**
- BUY: when predicted price > opening price
- SELL: when predicted price < opening price
- HOLD: when predicted price = opening price

✨ **Historical Analysis**
- 60-day price charts
- Interactive visualizations
- Price statistics (min, max, latest)
- Volume analysis
